George Orwell, Animal Farm's Author

Born Eric Arthur Blair, George Orwell was an English journalist and novelist. Born the 25th of June, 1903, in British India, he wrote novels that heavily influenced popular culture such as 1984 and Animal Farm. He served in the Spanish Civil War, on the Republican's side. Although he did heavily criticize the USSR, Orwell was a democratic socialist. He passed away January 21st, 1950.
